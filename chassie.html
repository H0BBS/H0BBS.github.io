What is it? This is the main body of the computer and it's where all the parts are put into. What's its purpose? The Chassis purpose is to house and protect the parts of the computer. What is it made of? Metal and plastic, often black in colour. How to choose? If you’ve chosen a motherboard then you need to get a chassi for that size, the most common sizes are,ATX, MicroATX, and Mini-ITX. These are motherboard sizes but they decide over chassi sizes. Other than that you need to pick one that can fit what you want in it when it comes to things like cooling and overall prestanda.
