CPU- What is it? CPU or central processing unit. It's the brain of the computer. What's its purpose? It's the process allowing us to run programs, without it we wouldn't be available to run our applications. What is it made of? Like most other computer parts it's made of silicon. How to choose? This is what determines your computer's speed, so you need to depend on what you want your computer to be able to do, choose a cpu that's fast enough while also being within your budget.
